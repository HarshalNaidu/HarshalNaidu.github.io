Do you understand Digital?

2016-06-07

Digital, Revolution and Disruption. The three words evoke fear and unease in most of the people unless of course you are surfing the wave.

This blog will explore the broad term digital and peek into its multiple facets. Whether we call this a revolution or worry about the disruption, I promise that by the time I have created the series of posts, you’ll have a good understanding of the big picture and if you are into business of creating and selling business software how you may want to think about the tech and your career.

I invite you to participate and educate the readers (including me) by sharing your perspective.

Let’s get started with the first post in the series and get to a “platform” from where we’ll go into tech, business models, career impact and more!
